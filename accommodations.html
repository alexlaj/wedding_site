Accommodations

We recommend staying at either Bear Lodge or Deer Lodge, two lovely, modern hotels located at the top of Mt. Washington and only a five-minute drive to Raven Lodge.

Wedding guests receive 10% off a 2+ night stay, or a waiver for the $25 one-night fee. Make your reservation for "Megan and Alex's wedding" using the promo code BLISS1 (one night) or BLISS2 (two or more nights). Book by phone 1 877 845 4499 or online.

Bear and Deer Lodge - rooms with rates for wedding guests

Lodge Room

Sleeps up to two guests. One queen bed, shower ensuite, some rooms have mini fridge and microwave. Washer/dryer in hallway.

One Bedroom Suite

Sleeps up to four guests. Full kitchen, one queen bed in bedroom, pull-out sofa, fireplace, balcony/patio, soaker tub. Washer/dryer in hallway.

Two bedroom Suite

Sleeps up to six guests. Full kitchen, one queen bed in each bedroom, pull-out sofa, fireplace, balcony/patio, soaker tub. Washer/dryer in hallway.

Three Bedroom Suite

Sleeps up to eight guests. Combination of a studio suite and two bedroom suite connected with a lock-off door.

Private Accommodations

A range of private rental accommodations are available on the mountain - condos, apartments, and private and multi-unit chalets (oh my!) These rentals are not eligible for discount for wedding guests. Browse the listings. Some units are also available through Air B&B.
